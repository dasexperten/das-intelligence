# Label Review Planner

## Purpose

Prepare product labels for market review by checking required fields, language, product facts, importer data, warnings, and layout notes.

## When to Use

Use this skill before printing labels, uploading product cards, sending artwork to partners, or entering a new market.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: country, language, product, label text, ingredients or specs, importer data, barcode, claims, icons, and package size.

## Workflow

1. Define market and product type.
2. Check required fields and language.
3. Review product facts, importer data, and barcode.
4. Identify missing or risky wording.
5. Prepare label change list and approval notes.

## Output

Always provide label review summary, required fields, missing items, wording notes, artwork notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Translating label without local review.
- Missing importer data.
- Missing package size.
- Inconsistent product facts.
