# Product Rules Planner

## Purpose

Plan what product rules must be checked before selling in a new market or channel.

## When to Use

Use this skill when entering a country, listing a product, preparing documents, or reviewing product category requirements.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: country, product category, product facts, ingredients or specs, channel, documents, labels, importer, and launch date.

## Workflow

1. Define country, product category, and channel.
2. List required checks and documents.
3. Identify missing product data.
4. Assign owners and deadlines.
5. Create launch readiness notes.

## Output

Always provide rule checklist, missing data, document list, owner notes, deadline notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Starting sales before product rules are checked.
- Missing importer role.
- Missing product facts.
- No owner.
