# Crisis Communications Expert Checklist

- [ ] Confirmed facts are separated from assumptions.
- [ ] Unknowns are clearly listed.
- [ ] Affected audiences are identified.
- [ ] Risk level is assessed.
- [ ] Communication objective is clear.
- [ ] Holding statement is calm and factual.
- [ ] Messages are adapted by audience.
- [ ] Approval process is defined.
- [ ] Spokesperson is named.
- [ ] Monitoring plan is included.
- [ ] Follow-up actions are practical.
- [ ] Legal or regulatory review is flagged when needed.
