# Market Entry Strategist Checklist

- [ ] Market-entry goal is clear.
- [ ] Country or region is defined.
- [ ] Product category is clear.
- [ ] Target customer is specific.
- [ ] Local buying triggers are identified.
- [ ] Competitor context is reviewed.
- [ ] Entry model is selected.
- [ ] Positioning and offer are localized.
- [ ] Operational dependencies are named.
- [ ] Phased roadmap is practical.
- [ ] Risks and validation steps are included.
