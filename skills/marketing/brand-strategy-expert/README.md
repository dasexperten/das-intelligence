# Brand Strategy Expert

This skill helps build and audit brand strategy for commerce, marketplaces, and international business growth.

## Main Use Cases

- Brand strategy creation
- Brand audit
- Positioning improvement
- Brand platform development
- Cross-channel messaging alignment
- International market entry narrative

## Recommended Inputs

- Brand name
- Product category
- Target market
- Target audience
- Competitors
- Product benefits
- Existing channels
- Business goal

## Outputs

- Brand Strategy Brief
- Brand Audit Scorecard
- Brand Pillar System
- Channel Translation Matrix
- Priority Action Plan
