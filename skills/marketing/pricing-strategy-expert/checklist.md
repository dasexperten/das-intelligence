# Pricing Strategy Expert Checklist

- [ ] Pricing goal is clear.
- [ ] Target customer is defined.
- [ ] Market or channel is named.
- [ ] Cost and margin constraints are considered.
- [ ] Competitor context is reviewed.
- [ ] Pricing logic matches positioning.
- [ ] Discount guardrails are included.
- [ ] Bundle or tier options are considered.
- [ ] Test plan has decision rules.
- [ ] KPIs connect to revenue and margin.
- [ ] Risks are identified.
