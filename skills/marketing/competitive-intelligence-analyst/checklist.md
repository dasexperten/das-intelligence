# Competitive Intelligence Analyst Checklist

- [ ] Category is clearly defined.
- [ ] Direct and indirect competitors are separated.
- [ ] Substitute options are included.
- [ ] Positioning is compared.
- [ ] Pricing and offers are compared.
- [ ] Claims are checked against proof.
- [ ] Customer review signals are included.
- [ ] Competitor strengths are respected.
- [ ] Weaknesses and gaps are identified.
- [ ] Recommended actions are prioritized.
