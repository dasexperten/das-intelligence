# Launch Campaign Planner Checklist

- [ ] Launch goal is clear.
- [ ] Success metric is defined.
- [ ] Target audience is specific.
- [ ] Key message and offer are clear.
- [ ] Priority channels are selected.
- [ ] Pre-launch, launch, and post-launch phases are included.
- [ ] Required assets are listed.
- [ ] Launch calendar is practical.
- [ ] KPIs are tied to phases and channels.
- [ ] Risks and fallback actions are included.
- [ ] Next steps are executable.
