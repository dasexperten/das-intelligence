# Growth Strategy Expert Checklist

- [ ] Growth goal is clear.
- [ ] Time horizon is defined.
- [ ] Main growth constraint is identified.
- [ ] Acquisition, conversion, retention, and monetization are considered.
- [ ] Quick wins and strategic foundations are separated.
- [ ] Experiments are prioritized.
- [ ] Decision rules are included.
- [ ] 30/60/90-day roadmap is practical.
- [ ] KPIs match the growth goal.
- [ ] Risks and dependencies are named.
