# AI SEO Optimizer

## Purpose

Prepare content and website structures that are easier for AI search systems, answer engines, and large language model summaries to understand, cite, and recommend.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to improve AI search visibility, answer-style content, entity clarity, expert pages, product explainers, comparison pages, or FAQ structures.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: brand, website, product category, target questions, content pages, entity facts, proof points, FAQs, expert credentials, schema, and target markets.

## Workflow

1. Identify entities, topics, questions, and proof points.
2. Review whether content answers questions directly and clearly.
3. Improve entity consistency, page structure, FAQ coverage, citations, and proof.
4. Recommend schema, internal links, and content formats that help machines and humans understand the site.
5. Prioritize pages that already have authority or commercial value.

## Output

Always provide AI SEO diagnosis, entity map, question map, content improvements, proof gaps, schema notes, internal link notes, and priority actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Writing only for bots.
- Creating shallow FAQ pages.
- Making claims without proof.
- Inconsistent brand and product facts.
- Ignoring normal SEO foundations.
