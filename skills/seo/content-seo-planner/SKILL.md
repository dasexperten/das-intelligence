# Content SEO Planner

## Purpose

Plan SEO content that matches search intent, supports business goals, strengthens topical authority, and connects informational content to commercial pages.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to create an SEO content plan, blog roadmap, topic cluster, content calendar, or search-driven editorial strategy.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: target market, language, keyword map, product categories, priority pages, competitors, existing content, brand expertise, and conversion goal.

## Workflow

1. Define SEO and business goal.
2. Group topics by search intent and buyer journey stage.
3. Identify content gaps and existing pages to improve.
4. Build topic clusters and internal link paths.
5. Create a prioritized content roadmap.
6. Add conversion and measurement notes.

## Output

Always provide content strategy, topic clusters, content roadmap, page mapping, internal link notes, KPI notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Publishing articles with no link to business goals.
- Creating content without search intent.
- Ignoring existing content.
- No internal link plan.
