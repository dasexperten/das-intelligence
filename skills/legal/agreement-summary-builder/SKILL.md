# Agreement Summary Builder

## Purpose

Summarize business agreements into clear practical points for management review.

## When to Use

Use this skill when summarizing distributor agreements, service contracts, supply terms, retail terms, cooperation notes, or draft agreements.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: agreement text, parties, date, scope, term, price, territory, obligations, renewal, termination, and open questions.

## Workflow

1. Identify parties, scope, and dates.
2. Extract key obligations and commercial points.
3. List risks, unclear points, and missing items.
4. Convert agreement into management summary.
5. Recommend next actions.

## Output

Always provide agreement summary, key terms, obligations, dates, risks, open questions, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Summarizing without dates.
- Missing obligations.
- Ignoring unclear points.
- No next action.
