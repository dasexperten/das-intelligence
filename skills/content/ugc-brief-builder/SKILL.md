# UGC Brief Builder

## Purpose

Create practical creator and UGC briefs that help people produce useful content without sounding over-scripted.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to prepare briefs for creators, affiliates, reviewers, short videos, product demos, testimonials, or social proof content.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: product, audience, platform, creator type, content goal, key benefits, proof points, must-say facts, do-not-say limits, deliverables, and deadline.

## Workflow

1. Define content goal and creator role.
2. Clarify audience, message, proof, and CTA.
3. Write deliverables and creative boundaries.
4. Add hook ideas, scene ideas, usage notes, and approval rules.
5. Keep the brief flexible and easy to follow.

## Output

Always provide creator brief, content goal, key messages, deliverables, hook ideas, proof notes, restrictions, and checklist.

## Common Mistakes

- Over-scripting creators.
- Missing product facts.
- No restrictions.
- No deliverable details.
