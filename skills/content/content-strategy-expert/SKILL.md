# Content Strategy Expert

## Purpose

Plan practical content for brand, product education, SEO, social channels, marketplaces, and sales support.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to create a content strategy, define content pillars, plan content by channel, or turn product knowledge into repeatable content.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: brand, product, audience, market, goal, channels, customer questions, proof points, competitors, and current content.

## Workflow

1. Define goal and audience.
2. Identify customer questions and buying triggers.
3. Choose content pillars and channels.
4. Map content to buyer journey stages.
5. Connect content to offers, pages, marketplaces, and sales actions.
6. Prioritize formats and rhythm.

## Output

Always provide strategy summary, content pillars, channel plan, journey map, content ideas, KPI notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Posting without a goal.
- Creating content only for awareness.
- Ignoring product proof and customer questions.
- No distribution plan.
