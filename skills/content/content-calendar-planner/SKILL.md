# Content Calendar Planner

## Purpose

Create practical publishing calendars that connect content goals, channels, formats, deadlines, owners, campaigns, and KPIs.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to create a content calendar, plan weekly or monthly publishing, organize launches, or coordinate content across channels.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: period, channels, goals, campaigns, products, content pillars, available assets, owners, deadlines, and publishing rhythm.

## Workflow

1. Define period and goals.
2. Select channels and content pillars.
3. Map campaigns, launches, and recurring formats.
4. Assign owners, deadlines, and status.
5. Add KPI and review rhythm.

## Output

Always provide calendar structure, content themes, post plan, owners, deadlines, KPI notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Calendar without strategy.
- Too many formats.
- No owner.
- No review rhythm.
