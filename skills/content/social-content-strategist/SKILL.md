# Social Content Strategist

## Purpose

Plan social content that builds attention, trust, education, product understanding, community, and sales support across social channels.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to plan posts, reels, shorts, carousels, social themes, or a social content calendar.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: brand, audience, channel, product, content goal, proof points, offers, trends, community questions, and publishing rhythm.

## Workflow

1. Define channel, audience, and goal.
2. Choose content pillars and formats.
3. Build hooks, messages, proof, and CTA logic.
4. Plan publishing rhythm and repurposing.
5. Define KPIs and next actions.

## Output

Always provide channel strategy, content pillars, post ideas, format notes, hooks, CTA notes, KPI notes, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Posting random content.
- No hook.
- No proof.
- No channel adaptation.
