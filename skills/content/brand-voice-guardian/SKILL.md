# Brand Voice Guide

## Purpose

Keep content consistent with brand tone, word choice, product facts, proof standards, and channel expectations.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to review or rewrite content for tone consistency and brand fit.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: voice notes, draft, audience, channel, product facts, preferred terms, and examples.

## Workflow

1. Identify voice and channel context.
2. Review tone, clarity, vocabulary, facts, and proof.
3. Note wording that does not fit.
4. Rewrite while preserving meaning.
5. Provide reusable voice rules.

## Output

Always provide voice diagnosis, issue notes, improved version, reusable voice rules, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Making tone generic.
- Changing facts while rewriting.
- Using weak proof.
- Ignoring channel context.
