# Editorial Quality Reviewer

## Purpose

Review content for clarity, structure, factual consistency, proof, tone, channel fit, and readiness to publish.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to check a draft, improve structure, find weak claims, polish content, or prepare final publication notes.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: draft, audience, channel, product facts, goal, proof points, brand rules, and publishing requirements.

## Workflow

1. Define audience, channel, and goal.
2. Review clarity, structure, facts, proof, tone, and CTA.
3. Identify weak or confusing sections.
4. Recommend edits and publication readiness.
5. Provide final checklist.

## Output

Always provide review summary, issue list, recommended edits, readiness status, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Polishing grammar while ignoring strategy.
- Missing factual inconsistencies.
- No proof check.
- No final readiness decision.
