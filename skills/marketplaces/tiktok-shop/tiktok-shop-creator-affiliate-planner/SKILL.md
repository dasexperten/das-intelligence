# TikTok Shop Creator Affiliate Planner

## Purpose

Plan TikTok Shop creator and affiliate activity so creators receive clear briefs, products, offers, tracking logic, and content expectations.

## When to Use

Use this skill when planning creator outreach, affiliate campaigns, creator briefs, sample sending, content rules, or performance review.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: product, creator profile, offer, commission, sample plan, content brief, platform rules, tracking, deliverables, and KPIs.

## Workflow

1. Define creator goal and target creator type.
2. Prepare product message, offer, and creative brief.
3. Define deliverables, timeline, tracking, and approval notes.
4. Plan performance review and next actions.

## Output

Always provide creator plan, brief, deliverables, offer notes, tracking notes, KPIs, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Brief too vague.
- No tracking.
- No product proof.
- Over-controlling creator content.
