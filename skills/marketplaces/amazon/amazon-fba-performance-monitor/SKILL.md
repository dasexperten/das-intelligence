# Amazon FBA Performance Monitor

## Purpose

Monitor Amazon FBA and marketplace performance so stock, fees, fulfillment, returns, campaigns, and margin risks are visible before decisions.

## When to Use

Use this skill when reviewing Amazon stock, FBA fees, delivery performance, returns, price changes, campaign results, or ASIN profitability.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: ASIN, price, cost, FBA fees, storage, campaign cost, returns, stock, sales, revenue, ratings, and listing readiness.

## Workflow

1. Review sales, stock, fees, storage, returns, and campaigns.
2. Identify margin risks and stock risks.
3. Compare base and promo scenarios.
4. Recommend price, stock, campaign, or listing actions.

## Output

Always provide performance diagnosis, margin notes, stock notes, risk drivers, scenario notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Looking only at sales.
- Ignoring FBA fees and storage.
- Missing stock risk.
- Mixing ASIN performance.
