# Marketplace Launch Manager

## Purpose

Plan and manage marketplace product launches so content, price, stock, reviews, ads, promotions, ranking signals, and operations are ready before launch.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to launch a SKU, enter a new marketplace, relaunch a product, or prepare a 30/60/90-day marketplace launch plan.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, product, category, launch date, stock, price, listing content, images, ads budget, promotion plan, competitors, logistics, and launch goal.

## Workflow

1. Define launch goal and success metric.
2. Check listing, content, stock, logistics, price, ads, and promotion readiness.
3. Build pre-launch, launch, and post-launch actions.
4. Prioritize ranking and conversion signals.
5. Define KPIs, risks, and fallback actions.

## Output

Always provide launch readiness diagnosis, launch calendar, asset checklist, promotion plan, ads plan, ranking actions, KPIs, risks, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Launching with weak content.
- Launching without stock buffer.
- Running traffic before conversion is ready.
- No post-launch optimization plan.
- No success metric.
