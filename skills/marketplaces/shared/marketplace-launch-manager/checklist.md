# Marketplace Launch Manager Checklist

- [ ] Launch goal is clear.
- [ ] Success metric is defined.
- [ ] Listing content is ready.
- [ ] Images and video are ready.
- [ ] Stock and logistics are ready.
- [ ] Price and promotion are planned.
- [ ] Ads plan is ready.
- [ ] Ranking actions are defined.
- [ ] Risks and fallback actions are included.
