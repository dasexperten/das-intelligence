# Marketplace Ranking Expert

## Purpose

Diagnose and improve marketplace ranking factors such as relevance, conversion, sales velocity, availability, reviews, price, content quality, ads, and operational signals.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to improve search position, category visibility, launch ranking, or sales velocity on a marketplace.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, SKU, category, ranking data, sales data, conversion rate, reviews, price, stock, ads data, competitors, and content quality.

## Workflow

1. Identify ranking goal and target query or category.
2. Review relevance, conversion, sales velocity, review quality, price, stock, and ads support.
3. Identify weak ranking signals.
4. Recommend content, pricing, ads, promotion, review, and stock actions.
5. Prioritize by impact and speed.

## Output

Always provide ranking diagnosis, weak signals, competitor context, priority actions, expected effect, and KPI notes.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating ranking as only keyword optimization.
- Ignoring conversion and sales velocity.
- Running ads while stock or listing quality is weak.
- Not comparing against direct category competitors.
