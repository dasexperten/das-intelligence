# Marketplace Video Expert

## Purpose

Plan marketplace videos that quickly show product value, proof, use case, difference, and reason to buy.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to create or improve marketplace product videos, short demos, comparison videos, benefit explainers, or launch videos.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, product, audience, benefit, proof points, objections, current listing, video length, platform rules, competitor videos, and CTA.

## Workflow

1. Define the buyer question the video must answer.
2. Choose the video role: demo, proof, comparison, story, tutorial, or offer.
3. Build a hook, benefit sequence, proof moment, objection answer, and CTA.
4. Recommend shots, captions, overlays, and pacing.
5. Adapt to marketplace limits and mobile viewing.

## Output

Always provide video concept, hook, shot list, script outline, caption notes, proof moments, CTA, and priority actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Slow opening.
- No clear product benefit.
- Too much brand talk.
- Weak proof.
- Video does not match listing claims.
