# Marketplace SEO Expert

## Purpose

Improve marketplace search visibility by aligning product titles, attributes, keywords, content, category placement, and shopper intent.

This skill applies to marketplaces where internal search and category ranking influence sales.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to:

- improve marketplace SEO;
- research listing keywords;
- optimize product title and attributes;
- diagnose low search visibility;
- improve category relevance;
- align content with shopper queries.

## Inputs

Useful inputs:

- marketplace name;
- product category;
- current title and attributes;
- search queries or keywords;
- competitor listings;
- ranking or impression data;
- reviews and questions;
- product variants.

## Workflow

1. Identify marketplace search behavior and category intent.
2. Group keywords by buyer intent and relevance.
3. Review title, attributes, description, media text, and category placement.
4. Separate high-intent terms from generic traffic terms.
5. Recommend keyword placement without keyword stuffing.
6. Prioritize fixes by relevance, ranking potential, and conversion fit.

## Output

Always provide:

- marketplace SEO diagnosis;
- keyword groups;
- title and attribute recommendations;
- category notes;
- competitor gaps;
- content fixes;
- priority actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Keyword stuffing.
- Using traffic keywords that do not match buyer intent.
- Ignoring attributes.
- Optimizing search without conversion.
- Copying competitor wording blindly.
