# Marketplace Pricing Expert

## Purpose

Plan marketplace pricing that balances competitiveness, margin, promotions, platform fees, logistics, discounts, and perceived value.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to review price, plan discounts, compare competitor prices, protect margin, or prepare promotional pricing.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, product, current price, cost, commission, logistics cost, competitor prices, promotion calendar, target margin, and sales goal.

## Workflow

1. Define pricing goal: margin, volume, ranking, launch, or promotion.
2. Review costs, commissions, logistics, and discount mechanics.
3. Compare competitor price ladders and perceived value.
4. Identify pricing risks and guardrails.
5. Recommend base price, promo price, bundle logic, and test plan.

## Output

Always provide pricing diagnosis, competitor context, margin notes, base price logic, promotion guardrails, risks, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Discounting without margin guardrails.
- Copying competitor prices without value context.
- Ignoring marketplace fees and logistics.
- Running promotions without stop rules.
