# Marketplace Questions Expert

## Purpose

Analyze and answer marketplace customer questions in a way that builds trust, removes purchase friction, improves listing content, and reveals missing information.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to answer buyer questions, analyze marketplace Q&A, create FAQ improvements, or identify content gaps from customer questions.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, product, customer question, product facts, listing content, reviews, policies, and claim limitations.

## Workflow

1. Identify the real concern behind the question.
2. Answer directly and factually.
3. Add useful product context without overclaiming.
4. Identify whether the listing should be updated.
5. Group recurring questions into FAQ or image improvements.

## Output

Always provide answer draft, buyer concern, proof or product fact used, listing gap, FAQ idea, and recommended update.

## Common Mistakes

- Giving vague answers.
- Making unsupported claims.
- Ignoring repeated questions.
- Answering only the literal question while missing the purchase concern.
