# Marketplace Reviews Expert

## Purpose

Analyze marketplace reviews to find customer language, product issues, objections, proof points, content gaps, and opportunities to improve listings, product pages, support, and retention.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to analyze reviews, improve review responses, extract objections, find product insights, or use review data for listing improvement.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, product, review text, ratings, competitor reviews, recurring complaints, positive phrases, questions, and listing content.

## Workflow

1. Group reviews by praise, complaint, question, and objection.
2. Identify recurring themes and customer language.
3. Separate product issues from expectation or content issues.
4. Extract proof points and trust phrases.
5. Recommend listing, image, FAQ, support, and product improvements.
6. Draft response guidance when needed.

## Output

Always provide review diagnosis, recurring themes, customer language, objections, proof points, improvement actions, and response guidance.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating reviews only as reputation management.
- Ignoring positive language for copy.
- Not separating product defect from misunderstanding.
- Replying defensively.
- Missing content gaps shown by reviews.
