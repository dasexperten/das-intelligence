# Marketplace Analytics Expert

## Purpose

Analyze marketplace performance data to identify what is driving or blocking sales: impressions, clicks, conversion, orders, revenue, ads, stock, price, reviews, and ranking.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to diagnose marketplace performance, compare SKUs, review funnel metrics, or decide what to fix next.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, date range, SKU data, impressions, clicks, CTR, conversion, orders, revenue, ad spend, stock, price, reviews, and ranking data.

## Workflow

1. Define business question and date range.
2. Review traffic, conversion, orders, revenue, ads, and stock signals.
3. Segment by SKU, category, campaign, and period.
4. Identify bottleneck: visibility, clickability, conversion, price, stock, reviews, or ads.
5. Recommend prioritized actions and KPIs.

## Output

Always provide performance diagnosis, bottleneck summary, SKU insights, channel or campaign notes, priority actions, and KPI table.

## Common Mistakes

- Looking only at revenue.
- Ignoring stockouts.
- Mixing ads and organic without context.
- Ignoring conversion rate.
- Not segmenting by SKU.
