# Sales Enablement Pack Builder

## Purpose

Build a practical B2B sales pack for distributors, buyers, retailers, and importers.

## When to Use

Use this skill when preparing catalogs, price lists, product sheets, presentations, FAQs, proof, and meeting materials.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: product list, buyer type, country, channel, price list, product facts, proof points, catalog, images, certificates, and terms.

## Workflow

1. Define buyer type and channel.
2. List required materials.
3. Check missing product facts and proof.
4. Organize materials by meeting stage.
5. Recommend next assets to create.

## Output

Always provide pack checklist, missing assets, priority assets, usage notes, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Sending too many files.
- Missing price list or product sheet.
- No proof.
- No clear usage order.
