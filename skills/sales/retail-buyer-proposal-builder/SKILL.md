# Retail Buyer Proposal Builder

## Purpose

Build practical proposals for retail buyers, pharmacy chains, supermarkets, beauty chains, and category managers.

## When to Use

Use this skill when preparing assortment proposals, buyer decks, category arguments, launch plans, or commercial summaries.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: retailer, buyer role, category, product list, price, margin notes, proof, competitors, launch support, logistics, and next step.

## Workflow

1. Define retailer and buyer goal.
2. Choose assortment and category argument.
3. Add proof, commercial notes, and launch support.
4. Prepare proposal structure.
5. Define next step.

## Output

Always provide proposal summary, assortment logic, category argument, proof points, commercial notes, launch support, and next step.

## Common Mistakes

- Sending only catalog.
- No category argument.
- No commercial summary.
- No launch support.
