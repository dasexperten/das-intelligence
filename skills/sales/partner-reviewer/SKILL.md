# Partner Reviewer

## Purpose

Review business partners before samples, pricing, territory, shipment, or long-term cooperation.

## When to Use

Use this skill when checking a distributor, importer, agent, retailer, or service partner.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: company, country, role, website, references, requested terms, payment plan, territory request, and past behavior.

## Workflow

1. Define partner role and requested decision.
2. Review identity, reputation, channel fit, payment reliability, and operational ability.
3. Identify missing information.
4. Recommend review level and next step.

## Output

Always provide summary, missing information, review level, next step, and approval notes.

## Common Mistakes

- Trusting first impression only.
- No reference check.
- Giving exclusivity too early.
- No written decision record.
