# Trade Show Meeting Planner

## Purpose

Plan trade show meetings so every conversation has a goal, prepared materials, qualification questions, and next action.

## When to Use

Use this skill before exhibitions, buyer meetings, distributor meetings, and booth conversations.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: event, country, target companies, meeting list, product focus, materials, samples, team roles, schedule, and next action.

## Workflow

1. Define event goal and target company types.
2. Prepare meeting list and materials.
3. Create meeting agenda and qualification topics.
4. Define sample and catalog handling.
5. Plan next action after each meeting.

## Output

Always provide event plan, meeting list, material checklist, agenda, qualification topics, owner notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- No meeting goal.
- No material checklist.
- No qualification topics.
- No post-event action plan.
