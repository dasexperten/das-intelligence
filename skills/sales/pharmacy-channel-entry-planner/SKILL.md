# Pharmacy Channel Entry Planner

## Purpose

Plan entry into pharmacy chains and pharmacy distributors with the right assortment, proof, terms, materials, and launch steps.

## When to Use

Use this skill when preparing pharmacy channel expansion, pharmacy buyer meetings, or pharmacy distributor proposals.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: country, pharmacy channel, product list, category, proof points, price, margin notes, required documents, competitors, and launch support.

## Workflow

1. Define pharmacy channel and buyer type.
2. Select suitable assortment.
3. Prepare proof, documents, and category logic.
4. Review price, margin, and launch support.
5. Recommend entry path and next actions.

## Output

Always provide channel plan, assortment logic, proof needs, document list, commercial notes, launch steps, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating pharmacy like normal retail.
- Missing documents.
- Weak category argument.
- No launch support.
