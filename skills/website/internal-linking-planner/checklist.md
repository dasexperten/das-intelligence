# Internal Linking Planner Checklist

- [ ] Priority pages are defined.
- [ ] SEO or conversion goal is clear.
- [ ] Page types are mapped.
- [ ] Topic clusters are identified.
- [ ] Orphan or underlinked pages are found.
- [ ] Content-to-product links are planned.
- [ ] Anchor text is descriptive.
- [ ] Links are relevant to user intent.
- [ ] Multilingual links are considered when relevant.
- [ ] Priority actions are practical.
