# FAQ Optimizer Checklist

- [ ] FAQ supports the page goal.
- [ ] Questions reflect real visitor doubts.
- [ ] Objections are answered clearly.
- [ ] Delivery, returns, payment, and safety questions are covered when relevant.
- [ ] Answers are concise and useful.
- [ ] Proof or policy links are included where needed.
- [ ] FAQ order follows buyer logic.
- [ ] SEO and schema opportunities are noted.
- [ ] Internal links are suggested.
- [ ] Next steps are practical.
