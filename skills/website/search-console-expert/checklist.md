# Search Console Expert Checklist

- [ ] SEO question is clear.
- [ ] Date range is defined.
- [ ] Top queries are reviewed.
- [ ] Top pages are reviewed.
- [ ] High-impression low-CTR opportunities are identified.
- [ ] Ranking opportunities are identified.
- [ ] Country and device segments are checked.
- [ ] Indexing notes are reviewed.
- [ ] Title and meta improvements are suggested.
- [ ] Internal linking opportunities are included.
- [ ] Priority actions are practical.
