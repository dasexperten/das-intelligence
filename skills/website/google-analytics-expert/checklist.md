# Google Analytics Expert Checklist

- [ ] Business question is clear.
- [ ] Date range is defined.
- [ ] Conversion goal is known.
- [ ] Traffic sources are reviewed.
- [ ] Landing pages are analyzed.
- [ ] Channel quality is compared.
- [ ] Device segments are checked.
- [ ] Country or language segments are checked when relevant.
- [ ] Tracking gaps are noted.
- [ ] Actions are tied to metrics.
